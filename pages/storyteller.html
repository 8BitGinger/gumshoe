Story Teller

Info

You will have to present clues for the players to follow. It's a touch balance to find the right difficulty level for your clues, hidden items, and red herrings. Nothing hurts more than your players completely unable to solve or progess the game and it's very frustating for them to have everything handed to them. Work with your players to find the right balance.

Clue Gameplay is based on a Percentile D100 Roll:
Unmissable Clue - 0 - 25%
Easy Clue - 26 - 50%
Moderate Clue - 51 - 75%
Hard Clue - 76 - 100%
