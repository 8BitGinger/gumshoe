Story Teller

Info

Gumshoe is a detective game set in a neon-noir city. The game is designed to be played by friends cooperatively as a TTRPG.

The Story Teller is the person who creates the world of Gumshoe. They are responsible for creating the setting, the characters, and the story that the players will interact with. The Story Teller will also be responsible for creating the mystery that the players will solve, and for creating the clues and red herrings that the players will find.
